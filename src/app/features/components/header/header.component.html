In sharing your data's metadata, you can:
  • Meet the requirements of the Data Provenance Standards expected by your data customers and consumers.
  • Automate the generation of metadata, reducing manual errors and saving valuable time.
  • With standards-compliant metadata, share your data with confidence, knowing it adheres to sound data governance practices.
Learn more about the Data Provenance Standards at https://dataandtrustalliance.org/our-initiatives/data-provenance-standards
How it works
01 Input your data. Fill in the required fields with your dataset information.
02 Choose your format. Select from JSON, CSV, XML, and YAML formats depending on your needs or system compatibility.
03 Generate and download. With a simple click, generate the metadata file and download it immediately for use.
